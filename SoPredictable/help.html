Help

This Shiny App is a simple type-ahead predictor. Just type in the input field and it will suggest a probable next word. Click on one of the suggested terms and it will be added to your input. If the suggestion is made in the middle of a word (indicated by a different button color) your word will be completed. You can click on the input again to continue typing or edit your input in any way you want. Caveat: prediction will only act on the right-end, not where you type.

Click on the Clear button to reset your input.

The number of suggested terms is capped to the 8 most probable terms of the predicted set.

Word Charts

The Word Charts tab will display a word cloud or a probability table of all terms in the predcition set (probability is normalized for the set), not just the suggested buttons. You can toggle between chart formats. The charts are a good indicator the relative porbability of the terms suggested.

Inner Data

The Inner Data tab will show the calculations for the predicted set in each of the levels of the algorithm, or it will indicate that the algorithm is guessing based on context terms.

Context Boost

When the context toggle is selected, the prediction set is altered based on the context provided by the entire input stream and not just the last three tokens. For the effect to be apreciated use terms with heavy influence (i.e. congress, committee, government, ...) in long input streams to provide enough context.
